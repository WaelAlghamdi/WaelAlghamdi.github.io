Wael Alghamdi

Information theory, fair machine learning, differential privacy

Wael Alghamdi received the S.B. degree in mathematics from Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Cambridge, MA, USA, in 2014, and the M.S. degree in electrical engineering from the King Abdullah University of Science and Technology, Thuwal, Saudi Arabia, in 2016. Since 2017, he has been a graduate student with the John A. Paulson School of Engineering and Applied Sciences, Harvard University, Cambridge, MA, USA. His current research interest focuses on information theory. In his free time, he enjoys random walks through the streets of Boston.
